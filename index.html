Rebrowse Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2025

1. What Data We Collect ➕

Rebrowse tracks the domain name of websites you visit and how much time you spend on each one. This data is collected locally on your device.

2. How We Use This Data ➕

This data is used solely to display browsing statistics in the Rebrowse popup. We do not use this data for any other purpose.

3. Data Storage ➕

All data is stored using the browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local). It never leaves your device and is never sent to any remote server.

4. No Personal Information Collected ➕

Rebrowse does not collect or store any personal information, including: your name, email address, IP address, location, or browsing content.

5. Third-Party Sharing ➕

Rebrowse does not share any data with third parties.

6. Contact ➕

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at goharshinwari15@gmail.com.

Rebrowse is committed to privacy-first design and local-only data processing.
